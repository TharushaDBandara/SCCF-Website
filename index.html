About Us

Our History

Founded in 2022, the Social Community Contribution Foundation emerged from a grassroots movement of dedicated individuals committed to creating lasting positive change in communities.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to the resources, education, and opportunities needed to thrive sustainably and equitably.

Our Mission

To create a suitable environment to provide young people with practical and theoretical knowledge of the rights and duties of a citizen of Sri Lanka, and to educate the entire citizenry on these rights and duties.

Our Aims

To build coordination and communication between government institutions to solve problems related to the rights of citizens, focusing on educating young people about their civic rights and duties.

To improve the political literacy of young people. To create young political leaders and contribute to the creation of youth political organizations.
